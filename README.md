# Audit Training Report – Under Anbarasu and Jalapathi Chartered Accountant

This repository contains my **Audit Training Report** completed under the guidance of Anbarasu and Jalapathi Chartered Accountant. The training provided practical exposure to accounting, auditing procedures, taxation processes, financial documentation, and professional business practices followed in a Chartered Accountant firm.

The training focused on understanding real-time auditing activities and financial operations performed for clients across different business sectors. It helped in gaining hands-on experience in analyzing financial records, maintaining compliance, and supporting audit-related documentation.

* Financial statement analysis
* Audit documentation and verification
* GST and taxation basics
* Voucher and invoice checking
* Ledger and accounting review
* Bank reconciliation process
* Client data management
* Accounting standards and compliance
